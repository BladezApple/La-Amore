Privacy Policy

At La Amore, your privacy is a top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, make a purchase, or contact us.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

● Your name and email address (when filling out contact or newsletter forms)

● Your shipping and billing address (during purchases)

● Device and browser data (automatically collected for analytics)

● Any information voluntarily submitted through messages or contact forms

How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

● Respond to your inquiries or requests

● Process and fulfill orders

● Send transactional and promotional emails (only if you opt in)

● Improve website functionality and customer experience

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties, except as required to complete services (e.g., shipping or payment processors).

Email Communication

If you sign up for our newsletter or submit a message through our contact form, we may send occasional updates or responses. You can unsubscribe from promotional emails at any time using the link provided in the message.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your personal data. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of information transmitted online.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party tools such as Formspree (for contact forms), Stripe or PayPal (for payments), and analytics services. These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies separately.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance browsing, track usage data, or remember your preferences. You can modify your browser settings to block or delete cookies at any time.

User Rights

You have the right to:

● Access or request a copy of your personal data

● Request correction or deletion of your data

● Withdraw consent for marketing communications

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@laamore.store
